What is computer science?

Computing is part of everything we do. Computing drives innovation in engineering, business, entertainment, education, and the sciences and it provides solutions to complex, challenging problems of all kinds.

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems. It is a broad field which includes everything from the algorithms that make up software to how software interacts with hardware to how well software is developed and designed. Computer scientists use various mathematical algorithms, coding procedures, and their expert programming skills to study computer processes and develop new software and systems.

Computer science is the field that explores the theory, development, and application of computational systems and technology. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, from algorithms and data structures to artificial intelligence, machine learning, software engineering, and cybersecurity. By studying how computers process information and solve complex problems, computer scientists design systems that drive innovations in industries such as healthcare, finance, and entertainment.

The field is also foundational to the development of technologies like the internet, mobile applications, and modern computing hardware. As technology continues to evolve, computer science remains central to shaping the future of how we interact with information and technology.
